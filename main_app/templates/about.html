About the UniMate

How Could We help you?

Find Your Ideal Room:Tired of spending countless hours searching for suitable student housing options? With CalUnimate, you can effortlessly browse through a curated list of available shared rooms and apartments near your university.

Connect with Potential Housemates: Finding the right housemate can make all the difference in creating a comfortable and enjoyable living environment.

List Your Room for Rent: Have a spare room to offer? As a user, you have the option to list your available room for rent on CalUnimate.Including rent amount, amenities, and any specific requirements you might have for potential tenants.

Share Amenities and Property Features: CalUnimate understands that student living is about more than just a room; it's about the entire experience. With our platform, you can showcase the amenities your property provides, whether it's high-speed internet, laundry facilities, a gym, or a cozy communal lounge. Highlighting these features helps potential tenants make informed decisions about their future home.

Explore Rental History: As a user, you can access the rental history of a property, giving you insight into its past occupants and their experiences.
